# Summary

This curriculum is a complete, structured education in **metaphysics**, using globally accessible online courses and authoritative texts. It aims to match the breadth and depth of a traditional four-year undergraduate major (minus non-major general education), but condensed into a sustainable 1–2-year plan for independent learners.

The philosophy is pragmatic and academic: metaphysics is best learned through immersion in logic, epistemology, philosophy of science, and the historical development of metaphysical problems. You get a balanced mix of readings, lectures, problem sets, structured writing, and self-directed projects.

---

## Organization

The program is organized in four phases:

1. **Intro to Philosophy & Study Foundations**
   Orientation, reading and writing skills, core philosophical habits, and short surveys of major areas including metaphysics.

2. **Core Metaphysics Curriculum**
   Systematic study of logic, epistemology, philosophy of science, modern metaphysics, ancient & early modern philosophy, and metaphysical method.

3. **Advanced Metaphysics Tracks** (choose one)
   Options include:
   • Metaphysics & Mind
   • Metaphysics & Science
   • Classical/Continental Metaphysics
   • Metaphysics of Mathematics & Modality

4. **Capstone Project**
   A substantial research paper or research portfolio suitable for peer review.

---

## Duration

Designed for **1–2 years at ~20 hours/week**.
A typical pace:

* Intro: 2–3 months
* Core: 8–14 months
* Advanced Track: 2–3 months
* Capstone: 1–2 months

# Capstone Project

The capstone synthesizes your chosen focus and demonstrates mastery.

### Goal

Produce a **15–25 page research paper** or an equivalent **portfolio of 2–3 shorter papers** on a metaphysical question. Designed to be seminar-quality and suitable for peer review in an online academic community.
